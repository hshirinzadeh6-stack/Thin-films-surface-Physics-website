  XPS - X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy or ESCA - Electron Spectroscopy for Chemical Analysis
XPS was developed in the mid 1960s by K. Siegbahn and his research group. K. Siegbahn was awarded the Nobel Prize for Physics in 1981 for his work in XPS.
